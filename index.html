Emotion axis

Does this mood feel good, bad, or uncertain? Cheerful = positive. Horror = negative. Mysterious = neutral.

Energy axis

Is the mood loud like a scream or quiet like a whisper? Dynamic = high. Peaceful = low.

Visibility axis

Does the mood reveal or conceal? Bleak shows everything. Ominous hides it. Certain = visible. Uncertain = obscured.

All three must align

One misaligned aspect undermines the whole. Lighting, Color, and Composition must reinforce the same mood.
